Welcome to my GitHub repository for the JHU PMB Computational Graphics Club!

The code and files in this repository can be used to follow along with our presentations. Feel free to download the items here and play with them at your leisure. For the R/ggplot stuff, there is a nice html format of the demonstration included. You can open it in RStudio and then click "Run Document" (RStudio may have to download some packages). You can also find it online.
